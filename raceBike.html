Trek Emonda

This is my race bike. I built it from the following parts:

  • Frame

    • 62cm Trek Emonda ALR

  • Drivetrain

    • Dura-Ace Front Derailleur
    • Ultegra 52-36, 175mm Crankset, 11-32 Cassette
    • 105 Brakeset, Shifters, Rear Derailleur

  • Wheels

    • Bontrager Paradigm Elite Wheelset
    • Michelin Krylion Endurance 2 Tires

  • Misc

    • Bontrager Montrose Elite Saddle
    • Bontrager Elite 110mm Stem
    • Bontrager VR-C 420mm Handlebars
    • VP Components VX Race Pedals
